Real Japanese food

Ramen

Each region of Japan has it's own flavour of ramen. From the thick miso ramen of Hokkaido to the cloudy white tonkotsu ramen of Kyushuu. There is a lot more to ramen than the instant variety.

Udon

Udon can be served hot or cold and different toppings are used at different times of year. Like ramen there are different flavours of ramen from each region of Japan.
